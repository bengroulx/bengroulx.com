Bonjour. Thanks for stopping by. How is your  going?

I am digital designer and creative strategist helping teams plan out software releases and improve their tech-building processes. This often means producing design systems and pattern libraries, UX reports, interface prototypes, and brand identity guides.

I take on projects with clients through House House, a shared design practice with fellow freelancers, and Briteweb, a social impact agency. For more information, see my CV / résumé.

For almost 30 years I’ve lived, worked, and played in the unceded lands of the Lil̓wat7úl, N’Quat’qua, Sḵwx̱wú7mesh, xʷməθkʷəy̓əm, Səl̓ilwətaɁɬ, Xwsenəč, and Lək̓ʷəŋən peoples. This remarkable area has helped shaped my priorities and influenced many of my works.

When I’m not at my desk in Victoria, BC, you might find me tending to an extensive indoor garden, or maybe reading with my partner Raquel D. and our daughter.
