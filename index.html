I’m Ben, a freelance digital designer and strategist. For 12 years I’ve helped teams plan out software releases and improve tech-building processes, often by producing design systems and pattern libraries, UX reports, interface prototypes, and brand identity guides.

Since 2017 I’ve taken on client projects through House House, a shared design practice with fellow freelancers. Most recently I’ve been working with a dedicated team from Briteweb to build a family of web-based products for College Advising Corps.

When not at my office in Vancouver, BC, you’ll find me tending to an extensive indoor garden or reading nonfiction with a pour-over in hand.
