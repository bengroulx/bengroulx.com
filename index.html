Hello there! Hope your is going well. I’m Ben G. I am a freelance digital designer and strategist.

For over a decade I’ve helped teams plan out software releases and improve their tech-building processes — often by producing design systems and pattern libraries, UX reports, interface prototypes, and brand identity guides. Since 2017 I’ve taken on client projects through House House, a shared design practice with fellow freelancers. Most recently I’ve been working with a dedicated team from Briteweb to build a family of web-based products for College Advising Corps.

I live and work in the unceded lands of the Lil̓wat7úl, N’Quatqua, Sḵwx̱wú7mesh, xʷməθkʷəy̓əm, and səl̓ilwətaɁɬ peoples. When not at my desk in Vancouver, BC, you’ll find me tending to an extensive indoor garden or reading nonfiction with a pour-over in hand.

Black lives matter. Always.
