Ben Groulx is a designer and art director in Vancouver, Canada.

For the past decade (or so) Ben has been helping the teams within larger organizations conceive, plan, and build digital products. Since 2017 Ben has shifted away from designing and developing projects for enterprises to guiding strategic efforts in service design and digital design for startups.
